AR Navigator

Where you are a click away from arriving at your destination.

Try It

Approach

Imagine, you are heading towards an airport station late, or that you are teaching a new instructee how and where majority of their operations will be done, or even if you find yourself wandering off in a hallway of a larger corporate office. What could possibly serve and aid you to arrive at your designated flight gate? Or layout the employees basic route routine for moving around boxes and materials? Or even just direct you to the nearest kitchen, bathroom, and nap space?

Solution

The fundamental principles behind AR Navigator is to make life easier for users with the use of directions configuring. Simply insert your current location, your desired destination, and watch the magic work as AR Navigator will direct you on where you want to go.

Takeaway

So whether it's airport and all you want to do is find your flight's entry point with ease, or simply layout a key for an employee to know when and where to head, and last not but not least know all the sweet spots inside a your company, allow AR Navigator to take you there.
